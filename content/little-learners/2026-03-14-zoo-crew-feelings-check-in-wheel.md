Children do not need a lecture when they are dysregulated.

They need a fast way to say what is true.

The Zoo Crew Feelings Check-In Wheel is built around that idea.

## What the tool does

- gives each common classroom feeling one Zoo Crew character
- gives children one sentence starter they can actually say
- gives teachers a local-only snapshot of the room by zone
- includes a printable wheel poster and matching check-in cards

## What makes a feelings tool usable

Most emotion charts are either too generic or too word-heavy.

This one keeps the move simple:

1. point to a feeling
2. say one sentence
3. get one next step

That sequence is short enough to hold up during arrival, after recess, or in the middle of a hard transition.

## Best classroom move

Use the same adult response each time:

- "Show me your feeling."
- "Say the sentence."
- "Here is your next step."

Children trust the tool faster when the adult response is calm and predictable.
