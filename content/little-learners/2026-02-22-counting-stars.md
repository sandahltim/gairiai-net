## For parents and teachers

**Counting Stars** is a simple counting activity designed for children ages 3-6. Stars appear on a night sky, and your child taps each one to count it. When they reach the target number, they get a little celebration.

### What it practices

- **One-to-one correspondence** — each tap counts exactly one star
- **Number recognition** — the target number is displayed prominently
- **Counting sequence** — reinforces the order of numbers 1-10

### Tips for use

- Let your child tap at their own pace
- Say the numbers out loud together as they tap
- Celebrate when they get it right — the game does this automatically with a little animation
- The target number changes each round, keeping it fresh

### Age range

Best for preschool (ages 3-5) and early kindergarten. Older kids may find it too simple, which means it's working — they've mastered counting!
