Transitions get noisy when the next step only exists in teacher talk.

The Zoo Crew Visual Schedule Builder is a free way to make the next move obvious.

## What the tool does

- starts with three practical templates: preschool day, kindergarten day, and morning routine
- lets you reorder the cards and rewrite each action cue
- pairs every block with a Zoo Crew character so the schedule feels visual, not generic
- prints cleanly for letter or A4 paper

## What makes a schedule actually work

Each card should do three jobs:

1. show one clear picture
2. use one short label
3. name one action

That is enough for children to scan quickly without waiting for a grown-up to explain the whole board again.

## Best classroom move

For the first week, point to the schedule before giving verbal directions.

After that, switch to:

- "Check the board."
- "What is next?"
- "Show me with your finger."

The board starts working when adults use it consistently, not when it looks perfect.
