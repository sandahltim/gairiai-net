Remember when art came in 8-bit chunks? This Pixel Art Studio brings back that retro charm with modern fluidity. Draw sprites, patterns, or just doodle on an infinite canvas that grows with your imagination.

The toolbar packs everything you need: a pixel-perfect pencil, instant bucket fill, eraser, and even line tool for precise shapes. Feeling fancy? Toggle symmetry mode to mirror your strokes horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — one stroke becomes four, and complex mandalas appear in seconds.

The palette editor lets you craft your perfect 16-color scheme. Pick hues, adjust saturation, save your favorites. When your masterpiece is ready, export as PNG for social sharing or ASCII art for that extra retro cred.

No accounts, no saves, no friction. Just open and create. Works beautifully on phones — sketch on the bus, refine on the couch, share from anywhere.
